Amateur Radio

All wireless communications began with radio. If it wasn't for radio, you wouldn't be seeing this, your new favorite site. Amateur radio = engineers playing around with electronics, radio frequency and equipment to see what can be done and also have fun. "Mess around and find out."

  • Radio gang [WØEEE]

    Antennas, RF, and computer talk only.
    We all hold extra class licenses and this semester we are all taking an Antennas course together📡😃.


  • Meeting Bob Heil at WinterFest 2022

    We met inventor, sound and radio engineer, Bob Heil, at WinterFest 2022! He later donated new equipment to the club and we share the same birthday. At the event, we tried to sell an old radio from the Vietnam War but it ended coming back home with me...$1k if anyone wants it.


  • Hanging out with Ward Silver — March 2022

    Author and engineer, Ward Silver, invited my friends and I to his main operating station. He has three giant towers and a lot of other cool stuff. He taught us how to safely climb a tower as well as some grounding and bonding basics.


  • WinterFest 2023

    Meeting and talking with multiple alumni was an absolute pleasure. It's always awesome listening to them share their incredible careers and the valuable advise that comes with experience. Also, I walked out of the convention with a couple of books that Ward Silver gave me for free, thank you Ward!
